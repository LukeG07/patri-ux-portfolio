#SIMplyDuolingo

This campaign shows kids and young adults from ages 16 to 23 that learning another language can be fun, easy, useful and non-time consuming! The Duolingo Owl will overtake one of the most beloved and popular videogames of all time, The Sims. This way no one will have to take time out of their busy lives to learn a new language!
