About Me

I'm a passionate multidisciplinary designer and illustrator based in Edinburgh, UK. With over 4 years of invaluable freelance work experience and a year in agency settings, including a branding consultancy agency and a digital agency, I bring a diverse skill set to every project.

Throughout my career, I have focused on strategy, user experience and visual development ensuring that every aspect of my work aligns seamlessly. I am genuinely enthusiastic about connecting with people and delving into their struggles, challenges, and motivations. By engaging in conversations and actively listening, I gain valuable insights that shape my creative process and allow me to deliver exceptional results.

In addition to my professional endeavours, I enjoy leveraging my skills as a freelancer to bring characters to life for independent projects or create captivating illustrations for a veterinary distribution company. When I'm not immersed in my work, you'll often find me indulging my inner video gaming nerd or exploring the great outdoors.
