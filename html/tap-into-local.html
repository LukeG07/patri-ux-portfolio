Tap Into Local

Digital Ad Campaign

Overview

We’ve been tasked with creating a interactive digital campaign that gets millennials into local shopping. We know they want to learn more about sustainable, authentic and eco-friendly brands that align with their own values. So, we’ve created Tap into Local – a quick and easy way for millennials to make new connections, discover more independent shops and learn more about the stuff they love. All with a tap.
